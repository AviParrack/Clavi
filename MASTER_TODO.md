## How to Use This File

**Adding a todo:** Claude adds it with date + Claude's score, prompts you for your score, computes average, assigns emoji.

**Format:** `| 🟡 | Task | Avg: X (You: Y / Claude: Z) | Added: YYYY-MM-DD | Notes |`

**Completing a todo:** mark ✅ in the Heat column, add completion date in Notes.
